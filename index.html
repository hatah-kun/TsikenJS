TSIKEN

Get ready for Tsiken, a 2D fighting game by Hatah Nicolas, inspired by Tekken. It's a solo player where you'll go 'beak to beak' with feisty chicken characters, and the goal is to emerge as the champion among Tsiken's feathered rivals.

MECHANICS
    CHOOSE CHARACTER
  • Select your fighter (the character you want to play with). Take a look at their stats board. Each character comes with its unique buffs.
    STATS
  • HEALTH: Represents your player's vitality, which decreases when taking damage.
  • ARMOR: Provides an additional layer of protection for your health, and it's the first line of defense against incoming damage.
  • MANA: Represents the energy your attacks require. The more damage you deal, the more mana you'll need. You can recover mana through DEFENSE and CHARGE moves.
  • BASIC ATTACK: Your standard attack, causing normal damage.
  • CRITICAL ATTACK: A special attack dealing heavy damage.
    CONTROLS
  • You can press the MOVE buttons below the stage or press key:
  • 1 - for BASIC ATTACK
  • 2 - for CRITICAL ATTACK
  • 3 - for DEFENSE
  • 4 - for CHARGE
    MOVES
  • BASIC ATTACK: A regular attack that doesn't consume much mana.
  • CRITICAL ATTACK: A powerful attack that requires a significant amount of mana.
  • DEFENSE: A blocking move to protect yourself from attacks while gaining extra mana.
  • CHARGE: Allows you to gain mana but leaves you vulnerable to attacks.
    NOTE
  • Attack buttons will be disabled when mana reaches zero.
  • A CRITICAL ATTACK inflicts damage(not full damage) on the opponent even if they are in DEFENSE mode.
  • If your mana is lower than what's required for a CRITICAL ATTACK, the damage is automatically converted to a NORMAL ATTACK.
  • DEFENSE moves are limited to five shield icons, decreasing when you use DEFENSE moves and increasing when you press other buttons.
  • After defeating an opponent, you will earn bonus points that can be distributed among your HEALTH, ARMOR, and MANA.
  • As you progress through the levels, your opponents will become progressively tougher.
  • Conquer the game by completing each level, facing off against seven challenging chicken opponents.
